# Advanced Features Quick Reference

## For Users

### How to Access Advanced Features

1. Open any **Poem** or **Blog** page
2. Look for the **⚙️ Settings button** in the header
3. Click it to open the Advanced Features modal

---

## Feature 1: Auto-Scroll (Like Live Lyrics)

**What it does:** Automatically scrolls the poem/blog content smoothly, like watching live lyrics.

**How to use:**
1. Click "Start Auto-Scroll" button
2. Use the **Speed Slider** to adjust scroll speed:
   - 0.5x = Very slow
   - 1.0x = Normal reading pace
   - 2.0x = Fast scroll
3. Click "Stop" to pause

**Best for:** Reading while listening to music, hands-free reading, following along with audio

---

## Feature 2: Read Aloud (Text-to-Speech)

**What it does:** Reads the poem/blog out loud in a natural, high-quality voice.

**How to use:**
1. Click "Read Aloud" button
2. Sit back and listen
3. Click "Stop" to pause

**Features:**
- ✨ Natural-sounding voice (not robotic)
- 📱 Works on phones and computers
- 🎧 Use headphones for best experience
- ⏱️ Reads at natural pace

**Best for:** 
- Blind/visually impaired readers
- Multitasking (listen while doing other things)
- Improving pronunciation
- People with dyslexia or reading difficulties

---

## Feature 3: Voice Dictation (Speak & Transcribe)

**What it does:** Listens to your voice and converts it to text in real-time.

**How to use:**
1. Click the **🎤 Microphone icon**
2. Speak clearly into your microphone
3. Watch your words appear as you speak
4. Click "Stop" when done

**Tips:**
- Speak naturally and clearly
- Works best in quiet environments
- Click "Clear" to start over

**Best for:**
- Voice-controlled reading
- Accessibility for those with mobility issues
- Hands-free note-taking
- Alternative input method

**Browser Support:**
- ✅ Chrome & Edge (full support)
- ✅ Safari (partial support)
- ❌ Firefox (not yet supported)

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't hear anything when I click Read Aloud"

**Check:**
1. Volume is turned up on your device
2. You have internet connection (needs ElevenLabs API)
3. Browser tab is not muted
4. Try a different browser (Chrome/Edge recommended)

### "Microphone doesn't work"

**Check:**
1. Browser has microphone permission
   - **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy → Microphone
   - **Safari:** System Preferences → Security & Privacy
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Use Chrome or Edge (best support)

### "Auto-scroll is jerky/choppy"

**Try:**
1. Close other browser tabs/apps
2. Reduce scroll speed
3. Restart your browser
4. Check your internet connection

## Tips & Tricks

### 💡 Best Experience

- **For Poetry:** Use auto-scroll + read aloud together for immersive experience
- **For Long Articles:** Start with auto-scroll at normal speed, adjust as needed
- **For Accessibility:** Combine voice dictation with read aloud for hands-free experience

### 🎯 Speed Settings

- **0.5x - 0.8x:** Slow, meditative reading
- **1.0x - 1.2x:** Normal reading pace
- **1.5x - 2.0x:** Quick scan/overview mode

### 📱 Mobile Tips

- Use landscape mode for better reading experience
- Keep volume on (not vibrate) for audio feedback
- Use headphones to avoid disturbing others
